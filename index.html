Vedang Waradpande

Data Scientist

I am a Data Scientist at Razorpay based in Bengaluru, India. I build Machine Learning models for detecting, and hence preventing, various kinds of frauds in the e-commerce ecosystem in India. My work here has involved building and deploying ML models for predicting if shipping addresses are deliverable and Return to Origin (RTO) orders. I also work on scaling ML architecture.

I worked on my undergraduate thesis from the Biomedical Informatics Lab at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My work there focused on applying Graph Neural Network-based models to the problem of Drug-Target Interaction and involved studying and experimenting with various kinds of Deep Learning models.

I graduated in 2019 from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS, Pilani) Goa Campus with a B.E. (Hons.) in Computer Science. During this time I also worked as a summer research intern at the Institute of Seismological Research, Gandhinagar; my project here involved predicting if the origin of ground motion signals was an earthquake or blasting activity. I worked on several interesting Machine Learning projects during my undergraduate program, the code for some which can be found here.

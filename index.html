Data Scientist

I am a Data Scientist at Razorpay based in Bengaluru, India. I build Machine Learning models for detecting, and hence preventing, various kinds of frauds in the e-commerce ecosystem in India.

I majored in Computer Science from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS, Pilani) Goa Campus. I worked on my undergraduate thesis from the Biomedical Informatics Lab at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My work there focused on applying Graph Neural Network-based models to the problem of Drug-Target Interaction and involved studying and experimenting with various kinds of Deep Learning models.
